Upgrade To Blocs 3

Come and explore the powerful new features of Blocs 3 and take your website design to the next level. There’s more to like than ever before, take a look.

How to upgrade

If you have a valid license for Blocs 2, you can upgrade to Blocs 3 at a special discounted price, saving you 30% off the retail price of Blocs 3. You can find more information about upgrading to Blocs 3 in this dedicated knowledge base post.

Please note, Blocs 3 uses the Bootstrap 4 framework. This means your Blocs V2 projects will need to be migrated to the new underlying framework. When upgrading a project from Blocs V2 to V3, Blocs will do most of the migration work for you, however, your project may require some additional work that will need to be done manually. You can read about migration between Blocs v2 and V3 here.

Free upgrades

You are entitled to a free upgrade to Blocs 3 if you purchased Blocs 2 after the 24th October 2018. Please read the information below to see which option applies to you. Please read the information below to see which option applies to you.

Option 1 - If you consented to receiving marketing information from us you will/should have received an email from us with details about your special upgrade offer.

Option 2 - If you did not consent to receiving marketing information, you will need to contact us here so we can issue your link.

(Please note, if when purchasing Blocs 2 you decided not to select to either consent or not consent, then you will be classed as not consenting and will need to contact us using option 2 above).
